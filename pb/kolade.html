Kolade Philip Ogunlana: A Multifaceted Creative

Kolade Philip Ogunlana, the author behind the captivating tale The Enchantress: Angela's Tale (ISBN 978-978-783-108-3), is not your typical creative individual. Beyond the lines of his intriguing storytelling, he navigates various realms, showcasing a diverse skill set that extends far beyond the realm of fiction.

A polymath in the truest sense, Kolade wears many hats - a writer, web designer, social media manager, motivational influencer, teacher, and a GitHub developer. His journey in the field of technology involves creating and deploying AI bots, as well as crafting websites that seamlessly blend efficiency and effectiveness.

His Instagram profile serves as a portal to his literary world, offering a glimpse into a collection of short stories, his latest book, and a treasure trove of unfinished narratives. Among his recent works in progress is "The Tale of Godot Thompson," a testament to his unwavering passion for fiction.

One cannot ignore The Enchantress: Angela's Tale, a must-read masterpiece that beckons readers to explore the depths of Philip's narrative prowess. The tale unfolds at this link, promising an experience that transcends the ordinary.

Educator and Advocate for Learning

Beyond his creative endeavors, Kolade emphasizes the importance of education in shaping successful lives. Advocating for informed citizenship, critical thinking, and productive societal contributions, he actively engages in private tutoring and education-related projects.

Currently involved with WiseGate Schools in the senior section, teaching English & literature, Kolade extends his knowledge to the next generation. His commitment to education aligns with his belief that it is a cornerstone for personal and collective success.
